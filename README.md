# Privacy Policy — Diurna

**Last updated: March 6, 2026**

Diurna (Daily Log, Research Edition) is designed to support research workflows while minimizing the collection of direct personal identifiers.

---

## 1. Data We Collect

- **Participant ID** — a pseudonymous identifier assigned by the research team; no name or contact information is stored alongside it.
- **Session & authentication metadata** — tokens and timestamps required to maintain a secure session.
- **Daily log entries** — text, structured fields, and optional media uploads related to study activities.
- **Device & app diagnostics** — crash reports and basic usage events used solely to maintain app stability.

We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or precise location data.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Data

Data is used exclusively to:

- Provide and maintain app functionality
- Synchronize participant logs to secure study infrastructure
- Support approved research analysis conducted by authorized research staff

Data is never used for advertising, profiling, or any purpose outside the approved study protocol.

---

## 3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade participant data.

Access is limited to:

- Authorized research staff named in the study protocol
- Service infrastructure providers required to operate the system, bound by data processing agreements

---

## 4. Storage & Sync

Entries are stored locally on the device first, then synchronized to a secure backend when a network connection is available. Backend services apply encryption at rest and in transit.

---

## 5. Security

We use role-based access controls, managed backend credentials, and standard operational security practices to reduce the risk of unauthorized access.

No system can guarantee absolute security. If you have concerns about a specific incident, please contact the research team directly.

---

## 6. Data Retention

Research data is retained according to the approved study protocol and applicable institutional policy. Upon study completion, data is archived or deleted per the project data management plan.

---

## 7. Your Rights & Data Deletion

Participants may request deletion of their data at any time by contacting the research team. Requests will be processed according to institutional policy and applicable law.

---

## 8. Children's Privacy

This app is intended for adult research participants only. We do not knowingly collect data from individuals under 18 years of age.

---

## 9. Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes materially, we will update the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
